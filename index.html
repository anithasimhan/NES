About NES

Two Decades of Environmental Excellence

Founded in 2002, Nandi Enviro Systems is a leading consultant and turnkey provider of industrial wastewater, sewage treatment, and air pollution control solutions..

Our services include in-plant surveys, effluent analysis, flow measurements, and design of optimal treatment systems, along with detailed layouts and cost estimates. We also support regulatory approvals, ensuring smooth compliance with Pollution Control Board requirements.

We are certified under a Quality Management System for the design, execution, operation, and maintenance of wastewater, sewage, and air pollution control systems.
What We Do

Our Core Services

End-to-end environmental engineering services — from initial assessment to full plant commissioning.

💧

Consultancy Services

Expert consultancy for water, sewage, and industrial effluent treatment. In-plant surveys, effluent analysis, feasibility reports, and full Pollution Control Board approval support.

🏭

Turnkey Execution

Complete project delivery from on-site survey to final commissioning. We handle design, procurement, construction, and clearances from Pollution Control Boards.

📋

Environmental Impact Assessment

Comprehensive EIA studies for proposed projects and expansion of existing Schedule I industries per MOEF EIA Notification 1994. Public hearing guidance and Environmental Clearance support.

⚗️

Hazardous Waste Management

Consultancy for hazardous waste storage and disposal as per MOEF, New Delhi guidelines. Covering service stations, hotels, industries, construction, and healthcare sectors.
